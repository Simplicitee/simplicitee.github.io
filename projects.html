ProjectKorra Abilities

ProjectKorra is a spigot minecraft plugin that takes the universe of Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra, and makes it a reality in minecraft. It includes all of the elemental bending, chiblocking, and has a few useful side plugins for things such as the spirit world, rpg, and the cool items/weapons they had.

ProjectKorra features an api that developers can use to make extra abilities and such, and I have made a plethora. Click the button to find out more about these.
